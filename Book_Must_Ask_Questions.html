Chapter 3: 60 QUESTIONS TO ASK


You should ask these questions to the self and to the franchisor. Until you get the satisfactory answers of these questions, it is recommended that you keep learning more and find out the answer of all the questions before getting into ATM franchise business:

1. Do we really need an ATM in our locality?

2. Why there is a need for an ATM in my locality? Can I justify the need?

3. Have I ever noticed the different make and model of ATMs in my locality?

4. Can I identify a full-fledged ATM or Cash Dispenser or a Cash Depository Machine?

5. Am I looking for a Real Passive Income Business?

6. Have you ever needed Cash and thought ‚Äì It would have been great if I had access to ATM right at my residential complex or shop?‚Äù

7. Did you ever feel to avoid travelling or avoid losing your valuable time and expenses to withdraw your Cash?

8. Did you ever think of any Passive Income?

9. Did you ever think of extra income without leaving your current job?

10. Did you ever think of utilizing your premise for some extra Profit?

11. Do I find any role of ATM in the locality where I live?

Chapter 8: WHAT TO CHECK BEFORE APPLYING


Before you apply for an ATM franchise, you must ensure that you have done the below:

# You have done enough research on your business location to ensure that it has high potentiality.

# You have checked your probable competitors.

# You have got answers to all your queries and all doubts clarified with the ATM operator.

# You have taken photographs of your location (site) and checked them many times to ensure that the proposed location has good visibility and cleanliness in the area.

# You have taken a 360-degree video of your location.

# You have the required fund to invest and run the business.

# You have a clear understanding of the business model or the concept.

# You have understood the risk factors.
